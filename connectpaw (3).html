How It Works

From panic to clarity
in under 15 minutes.

Every session is designed to move from first contact to a clear, actionable verdict — without delay, without cold starts.

1
Choose your path
Need a quick triage? Walk into Basic Care — available 24/7. Already know you need a vet doctor? Book Advanced Care directly, any time.
2
Complete Intake
14 structured fields capture everything before the session opens. Species, symptoms, medications, and a photo or video upload.
3
Connect with Your Vet
Your vet professional or AVS vet doctor joins with your pet's full history already loaded — no cold starts, no repeated explanations.
4
Clear Verdict
Stay home with care instructions, visit a clinic, or escalate to a vet doctor. Every outcome is documented in writing, instantly.
5
Recovery & Records
